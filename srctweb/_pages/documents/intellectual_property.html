Your intellectual property

Simply using our projects does not give you ownership of any of our intellectual property rights. However, because all of our code is available as free software, you are more than welcome to contribute to our projects. Even if you aren't comfortable contributing code, ideas or a keen artistic eye are always in need.

Our intellectual property

We're working on licensing with the Office of techology Transfer, but until the details are ironed out, your contributions to SRCT projects fall under the same rules as any other work you create at GMU.
