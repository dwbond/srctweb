Rules for using our services

Play Nice

Many of our services have APIs, and you can get backend access to our projects by coming to meetings for questions or help, so please don't try to access, disrupt, or otherwise interfere with our projects in a way we don't intend. It takes a lot of work to keep our projects running as it is. Because we are hosted on GMU machines, not only will you run afoul of Mason's Responsible Use of Computing policy, but you'll be hurting the open community we're seeking to create. We always need people to work on security, so if you're interested, please come out!
