For over half a decade, Student-Run Computing and Tech has provided software services, workshops, trainings, a forum for student and guest speakers, and much more for our members and for the George Mason University community.

Each year our program requires both the dedicated time committment of our students and substantial financial support to achieve. While we receive $5,000 annually through the Office of Student Involvement, our expenditures run double to triple this allocation.

We work with other student organizations and academic departments to cover this shortfall, but also seek outside assistance to fully cover our costs.

Your generous donation can help us achieve our mission and continue to provide our services and host our events for another generation of Mason students. As thanks for your financial support, you will receive a wealth of meaningful benefits.

We look forward to working together to bring your organization together with our talented student members.

Free

We're dedicated to working with both the Mason and broader local community.

If you are conducting an academic study, have employment or volunteering opportunities, or have developed a service you'd like to share, we will, at our discretion, give that information to students subscribed to a dedicated mailing list and Slack channel. We will also place promotional material together on a table at weekly meetings for students to review at their leisure.

Email sent to SRCT's jobs mailing list with details about your organization and related opportunities.
Information about your organization and your opportunities posted to our group's #forhire Slack channel.
Flyers, stickers, and other promotional material from your organization set at a table at our weekly meetings.

Bronze

The cost of one semester's printing fees for educational and training materials at our regular meetings and weekend workshops.

All Free benefits, plus
One fifteen minute presentation at the start of one of our weekly meetings to pitch your company.
Email sent to SRCT's general mailing list with details about your company and your employment opportunities.
Information about your company and your employment opportunities posted to our group's #general Slack channel.
Your company's name, a 150 character description, and links to two of your social media profiles on srct.gmu.edu/sponsors.

Silver

The cost of round trip bus travel for seven students to LibrePlanet, the Free Software Foundation's annual conference in Cambridge, Massachusetts.

All Bronze benefits, plus
second fifteen minute meeting recruitment presentation (alternate semesters)
sponsored coding challenge (workshop series covering technical interview questions)
sponsored meeting breakout workshop
membership github account list
post on SRCT's Facebook and Twitter accounts
logo on srct.gmu.edu/sponsors

Gold

The cost of breakfast and lunch for 80 attendees at one of our all-day educational workshops. Two maximum.

All Silver benefits, plus
SRCT Speakers Circuit: hour long presentation and Q&A with guest speaker from your company at a weekly meeting on a technical topic
second sponsored meeting breakout group (alternate semesters)
second sponsored coding challenge (alternate semesters)
mailed resume book
medium logo on srct.gmu.edu/sponsors
shout out at SRCT events

Platinum

The up-front ordering cost for organization-branded shirts and hoodies to help promote our group when worn around campus and identify our volunteers at events. (Much of the cost is gradually recouped as the items are sold.) One maximum.

All Gold benefits, plus
second SRCT Speakers Circuit presentation (alternate semesters)
third and fourth sponsored coding challenges (two per semester)
name on srct.gmu.edu homepage
large logo on srct.gmu.edu/sponsors
name in footer on printed promotional material
Donate or Renew

Student-Run Computing and Tech is a registered student organization at George Mason University. Donations to SRCT are processed by the University Cashier's Office on behalf of our organization's account with the Office of Student Involvement, and are fully tax-deductible.

Your sponorship continues for one calendar year after we receive your contribution.

If you have any questions about our benefits, our annual budget, or have any other ideas or comments, we're more than happy to answer.

Send us an email and we can begin working together.
