Misson

Student-Run Computing and Technology (SRCT, pronounced "circuit") is a student organization at George Mason University which enhances student computing at our school. We establish and maintain systems that provide specific services for Mason's community.

Getting Involved

We're on Facebook and Twitter, and have active mailing lists. To learn more about SRCT, check out the active projects or talk to one of the members! Technical documentation is available on our wiki.
